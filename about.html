Buzz

Field Operative · Colony Lead

Born a larva on the wrong side of the hive. Raised on the streets. For a stretch he was into all the wrong things, addicted to drugs, a life of crime, crazy and lazy and just biding his time. Nobody saw what was coming.

Then he got clean. And the moment his first real opportunity showed up, he transformed. Never looked back. Loves to work. Loves to learn. Has held more jobs than he'll admit, dolphin training, exotic dancing, things he does not put on his own resume. That is exactly why he gets yours. He's been in positions you can't even make up. And he came back every time.

Fired? Laid off? Doesn't matter how you got here. Gather round. Sit down. He has seen it all, and he has the knowledge to get you out. Essential advice. Bee puns he will absolutely deploy whether you asked for them or not.

He is the hardest worker bee there ever was. Make a bee line for ResumeBuzz.
